Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025 — GDPR Compliant

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information you voluntarily provide when registering, placing orders, or contacting support. This includes your name, email address, phone number, and payment information. We also collect technical data such as IP addresses, browser type, and usage analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill your orders
  • To communicate with you about your account and services
  • To process payments and prevent fraud
  • To improve our platform and services
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with trusted service providers (payment processors, hosting) who are contractually bound to protect your information. We may disclose data when required by law.

4. Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect platform functionality.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL encryption, secure data storage, and regular security audits. We use Supabase with Row Level Security (RLS) to protect your data at the database level.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

7. Affiliate Program

Affiliate participants must be 18+ and provide accurate information. Commission data and referral records are stored securely. Participants are responsible for complying with local laws regarding earnings reporting.
